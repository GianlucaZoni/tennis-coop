## Controls

General           | Keybind                        
----------------- | -------------------------------- 
Reset     | <kbd>r</kbd>

Player 1           | Keybind                        
----------------- | -------------------------------- 
Move forward      | <kbd>w</kbd>
Move backward     | <kbd>s</kbd>
Move left         | <kbd>a</kbd>
Move right        | <kbd>d</kbd>
Swing / Charge   | <kbd>Spacebar</kbd>  

Player 2           | Keybind                        
----------------- | -------------------------------- 
Move forward      | <kbd>↑</kbd>
Move backward     | <kbd>↓</kbd>
Move left         | <kbd>←</kbd>
Move right        | <kbd>→</kbd>
Swing / Charge   | <kbd>Enter</kbd>
